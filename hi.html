Real Life Inspirations

Ghazal Alagh

Founder, Mama Earth

She co-founded a toxin-free makeup and skincare brand that revolutionized the Indian beauty industry. Her journey from a mother seeking the best for her child to a leading entrepreneur showcases her vision, resilience, and business acumen. Through her brand, she empowers consumers with safer beauty choices while inspiring countless women to chase their dreams.

Upasana Taku

Co-founder of MobiKwik

Co-founder of MobiKwik which revolutionized digital payments in India, her relentless drive and innovative mindset have played a key role in making financial transactions seamless and accessible for millions. As one of the leading women in fintech, she has shattered stereotypes and inspired countless aspiring entrepreneurs.

Falguni Nayar

Founder, Nykaa

Founder of Nykaa, she transformed India's beauty and fashion landscape. Her vision and perseverance turned a startup into a billion-dollar empire, empowering countless women with beauty and self-care choices. As one of India's richest self-made women, she shattered glass ceilings and redefined success.
